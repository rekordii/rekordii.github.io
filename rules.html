Main Blackjack Rules

General

  • Every player plays against the dealer.
  • Goal: Get a higher hand than the dealer but below 22.
  • If you beat the dealer you win your bet. Otherwise you lose it.

Objective

  • Only beating the dealer can win a bet.
  • If a player's hand gets over 21 he loses through busting.
  • Card Values:
    • 2 through 10: Just the number on the card.
    • Jack, Queen, King: 10 Points.
    • Ace: 1 or 11 Points(what fits best).
    • Blackjack: 21 Points gained through only 2 cards.

Game Start

  • There are several Decks used for playing.
  • The dealer hands one card to every player
    and places one card face-down in front of him.
  • The dealer hands out a second card to everyone.

Main Game Action

  • The Player to the left of the dealer starts.
  • Take a look at your cards and decide if you hit or stay.
  • Staying: will close the round for you! You dont get another card.
  • Hitting: You will get another card and continue.

Game End

  • Every player finished (either busting or staying).
  • The dealer reveals their original card.
  • Dealer Actions:
    • If the total is 16 or lower: Hit.
    • If the total is 17 or higher: Stay.
  • If the dealer gets a Blackjack everyone loses.
